Header under tabs: Family Promise of Brevard, Inc. is a network of congregations helping homeless families in Brevard County.


Family Promise of Brevard, Inc. is an affiliate of a national program called Family Promise. Family Promise of Brevard, Inc. is striving to make a difference in the lives of the homeless families in Brevard County. Affiliates throughout Florida have GREAT SUCCESS in helping homeless families acquire permanent housing and gainful employment.

Close to 80% of the families participating in the Family Promise program transition into a safe and stable environment that brings structure into their family dynamics. Our case manager will provide a connection and some assistance to these families for one year after they are in their home.

While at Family Promise families stay together and the children are in a safe and stable environment that brings structure into their family dynamics. The parents do not have to worry about where they are going to find the next meal for their children or where they will stay each evening. Through case management, the families are connected to social service agencies. For example they assist them with job skills training, food stamps, day care assistance, housing leads, etc. Through our Interfaith Hospitality Network we connect people to people.

The families receive encouraging words and affirmation, share laughter with others, and find caring and concerned volunteers who brighten their day. Together as an Interfaith Hospitality Network we are building community and strengthening lives.
